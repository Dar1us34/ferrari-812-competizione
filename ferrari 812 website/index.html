About the Ferrari 812 Competizione

The 812 Competizione is the ultimate expression of Ferrari’s front‑engined V12 architecture. Its 6.5‑litre naturally aspirated engine produces 830 cv and revs to an astonishing 9,500 rpm.

Extensive use of carbon fibre, re‑engineered intake and exhaust systems, and a sophisticated aerodynamic package make the 812 Competizione one of the most focused road‑legal Ferraris ever built.

Rear‑wheel steering, advanced traction systems and a track‑oriented chassis setup ensure precision and confidence at the limit.
